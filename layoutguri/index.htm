Dec 05 2013 -
A Couple of Fixes
ith yesterday's server save, a few fixes were implemented.
  • We fixed an issue that announced the mini world change "Bank Robbery" with the same message as "Stampede" on the world board in the Adventurers' Guild.
  • We addressed a small issue with Lurik so that players are no longer stuck in the mission "The Island of Dragons" of the Explorer Society quest if they lost the scale from a frozen dragon.
  • The witches' grimoire can now be bought from Black Bert if you opened the chest in the mission "Eclipse" of the Inquisition quest.
  • A death in PvP through friendly fire is now correctly counted as PvP death so that a Twist of Fate blessing can take effect.
  • Levitating on magic walls and wild growth now results in the same effects as if a player had walked into them. Please note that the effects depend on the PvP mode setting of the player who placed the obstacle.
Have fun!
